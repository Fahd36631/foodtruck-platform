# 2. Ideas Explored

During the brainstorming stage, the team discussed several project ideas that solve real-world problems. Below are the ideas that were explored.

---

## Idea 1: Gym Marketplace Platform

### Description

A platform that connects gym owners with users looking for gym memberships. Gym owners can create accounts and display their gym information including:

- Subscription plans
- Services
- Photos
- Offers
- Gym locations

Users can browse gyms and filter them based on:

- City
- Subscription duration
- Services provided
- Price

### Reason for Rejection

Although the idea solves a real problem, we found that similar platforms already exist and the idea might not offer enough innovation for the project scope.

---

## Idea 2: Personal Trainer Marketplace

### Description

A platform that connects fitness trainers with clients who want personal training services.

The system would include three roles:

- **Trainer**
  - Create a profile
  - Upload certifications
  - Add services and pricing
  - Manage training offers

- **Admin**
  - Review and approve trainer applications
  - Monitor platform activity
  - Manage users

- **Users**
  - Search for trainers
  - Subscribe to a trainer
  - Track training plans and sessions

### Reason for Rejection

While the idea is useful, the mentor suggested that the concept may be too similar to existing freelance-style platforms.

---

## Idea 3: Farmer and Delivery Marketplace

### Description

A platform that connects farmers who produce crops with delivery drivers who transport the products.

Roles include:

- **Farmers**
  - List available crops
  - Provide harvest details
  - Set prices

- **Delivery Drivers**
  - Accept delivery tasks
  - Transport products

- **Admin**
  - Monitor transactions
  - Manage accounts

### Reason for Rejection

The idea was considered complex in terms of logistics and operations, which may exceed the scope of the project timeline.

# 3. Selected Idea (MVP)

## Food Truck Discovery and Ordering Platform

After evaluating the ideas with our mentor, the team selected the **Food Truck Platform** as the final MVP concept.

---

# 4. Problem Statement

Many high-quality food trucks offer excellent food but struggle with visibility. Customers often do not know where these food trucks are located or whether they have moved to a new location.

Additionally, users may visit an area expecting a specific food truck but fail to find it because its location changes frequently.

---

# 5. Proposed Solution

We propose a **Food Truck Discovery Platform** that helps users find food trucks in real time and order food easily.

The platform connects:

- Food truck owners
- Customers
- Platform administrators

The application helps promote food trucks and makes it easier for customers to discover and order from them.

---

# 6. How the Application Works

### User Experience

When a user opens the application, the main interface displays a **map showing available food trucks nearby**.

The user can:

- Select a neighborhood or location
- View food trucks operating in that area
- Filter trucks based on:
  - Food categories
  - Drinks
  - Meal types

Once a user selects a food truck, they can:

- View the menu
- Place an order
- Pay through the platform
- Receive an **order number**
- See the **estimated preparation time**

---

# 7. Food Truck Owner Experience

Food truck owners can register on the platform and provide information including:

- Food truck name
- Menu items
- Photos
- Location
- Operating hours
- Municipal license documentation

Owners receive **notifications when a new order is placed**, including the order number.

Once the order is ready, the owner can notify the customer through the platform.

---

# 8. Key Features

The main features of the platform include:

- Live location tracking of food trucks
- Interactive map for discovering trucks
- Menu display for each truck
- Order management
- Online payments
- Real-time notifications
- Filtering by food type and location

---

# 9. User Roles

### Users
Customers who browse food trucks, place orders, and track order status.

### Food Truck Owners
Business owners who manage their trucks, menus, and incoming orders.

### Admin
Platform administrators who manage users, food trucks, and platform activity.

---

# 10. Potential Challenges

Some challenges that may arise during development include:

- Real-time location tracking
- Managing live updates for food truck locations
- Handling payment systems
- Maintaining smooth communication between users and food truck owners

---

- Helps users easily discover food trucks
